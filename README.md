## ğŸ§  How It Works (GenAI Implementation)

At the core of MedSync AI is a carefully designed **Golden Prompt** implemented in **Google AI Studio**.

The Gemini model:
1. Analyzes patient vitals, symptoms, age, and context  
2. Assigns a triage level inspired by standard emergency protocols  
3. Evaluates hospital capacity, specialist availability, and ETA  
4. Generates explainable, structured recommendations  
5. Flags high-risk cases and safety overrides  

All decision logic is encoded through **prompt engineering**, without custom model training or fine-tuning.
